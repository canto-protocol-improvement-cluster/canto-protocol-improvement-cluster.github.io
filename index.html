About

Welcome to the Canto Protocol Improvement Cluster! We are a community of protocol engineers who are passionate about creating and improving protocols within the Canto community. Our goal is to provide resources, foster collaboration, and encourage innovation.

Resources

Our community offers various resources for protocol engineers, including:

  • GitHub Organization
  • Community Forum
  • Documentation

Join the Cluster

If you're interested in joining the Canto Protocol Improvement Cluster, please follow these steps:

  1. Create a GitHub account if you don't have one already.
  2. Join our Community Forum and introduce yourself.
  3. Explore our GitHub Organization and consider contributing to existing projects or starting your own.
