About

Welcome to the Canto Protocol Improvement Cluster! We are a community of protocol engineers who are passionate about creating and improving protocols within the Canto community. Our goal is to provide resources, foster collaboration, and encourage innovation within the field.

Join the Cluster

If you're interested in joining the Canto Protocol Improvement Cluster, please follow these steps:

  1. Create a GitHub account if you don't have one already.
  2. Join our Community Forum and introduce yourself.
  3. Join our Telegram and get involved in the conversation.
  4. Explore our GitHub Organization and consider contributing to existing projects or starting your own.

Canto Protocol Improvement Proposal (CPIP)

  • The Canto Protocol Improvement Proposal (CPIP) process is managed and maintained by a separate cluster of individual volunteers operating in the capacity of core contributors and maintainers.
  • Subjects for potential CPIP acceptance should be first discussed within the Telegram. Once sufficient discussion has been conducted and general consensus regarding the nature of the proposal has been achieved, including a general idea for mechanism design and implementation considerations, a forum post will be published on canto.build to provide a high-level overview of the proposed improvement.
  • Based on community feedback, a final CIP will be submitted as a PR to the Canto Core Protocol Implementation GitHub repository.
  • Each PR will be reviewed by at least 3 contributors. Each contributor should respond with appropriate comments to acknowledge review of each individual PR.
  • Should no changes be requested, a CIP can be considered ready for the implementation.
